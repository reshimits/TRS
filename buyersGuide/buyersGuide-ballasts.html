How much light output do you need?

35 Watt Ballast

Overall: More efficient, longer lasting. Industry Standard in all vehicles that come factory-equipped with HID headlights. Best for use on vehicles with Canbus systems or weaker than average electrical systems (old vehicles, motorcycles, etc). Lumens: 5800-6200 Lifespan: ~2,000 hrs Power Consumption: average Color Shift: none

55 Watt Ballast

Overall: Brighter, more power hungry, lesser lifepsan. Produces more output compared to all factory-installed HID headlight ballasts. Not recommended without the use of a relayed wire harness or in smaller projector and/or housings (such as fog lights). Lumens: 9100-9700 Lifespan: ~1,500 hrs Power Consumption: above average Color Shift: yes
